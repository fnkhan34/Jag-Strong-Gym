Our Story

Story

JagStrong Gym is a family-owned, Middletown-built training facility. No corporate playbook. No franchise polish. Just a real gym run by people who train, for people who train.

We took the old-school ethos — heavy basics, hard work, respect for the iron — and paired it with equipment and coaching that actually move the needle. Whether it's your first membership or your tenth prep, you'll be treated like family and pushed like an athlete.
